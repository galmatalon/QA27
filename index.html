The moment you set foot in Paris, you’ll feel transported onto a film set. Because the French capital has been largely undamaged by catastrophe or war, it has accumulated a wealth of historical monuments and buildings.
In fact, more than half of the buildings you see in Paris today were built before 1949. Picturesque parks are scattered throughout each quarter, offering refuge from the urban bustle.
